# Privacy Policy — Shiflow

**Last updated:** May 2026

Shiflow (“the App”) is used by restaurant staff and managers to view schedules, submit requests, exchange messages, and manage timecards.

## Information we collect

- **Account information:** name and credentials you use to sign in (authenticated through our server and Supabase).
- **Work-related data:** schedule assignments, time-off and swap requests, messages between staff and managers, and time clock punches stored in our database.
- **Device data:** standard technical data needed to run the app (network requests to our servers).

We do not sell your personal information.

## How we use information

- Operate scheduling, messaging, and timecard features
- Authenticate users and enforce manager vs employee access
- Improve reliability and support

## Where data is stored

Data is stored in **Supabase** (hosted database) and processed by our **application servers**. Data may be stored in the United States or other regions where our providers operate.

## Sharing

We share data only with service providers required to run the app (e.g. hosting, database), when required by law, or with your employer’s authorized administrators.

## Security

We use industry-standard HTTPS and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your choices

Contact your employer or app administrator to update roster information, correct punches, or deactivate access.

## Children

The App is not intended for children under 13.

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
