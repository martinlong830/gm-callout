# For managers

After you sign in, you will see five tabs at the top: **Schedule**, **Team**, **Actions**, **Messages**, and **Timecards**. Tap **Sign Out** (top right) when you are done.

## Schedule

Build and update the weekly schedule.

1. Choose **Week 1**, **Week 2**, or **Week 3**.
2. Choose a **location** (for example 9th Ave).
3. Tap any shift on the calendar to open that shift.
4. **Edit Staffing** — select who is working that shift, then tap **Save**.
5. **Coverage** — use when you need to find coverage for a callout (phone outreach to staff).
6. Use the toolbar when you need to:
   - **Templates** — apply a saved weekly pattern
   - **Shift Times** — edit default shift hours
   - **Manage Locations** — add or rename locations

**Role colors on the calendar**

- **Front of the House** (bartenders)
- **Back of the House** (kitchen)
- **Delivery / Dishwasher** (servers)

## Team

Your full staff list.

- Tap **+** to add someone new, or tap a name to open their profile.
- On each profile you can set: name, role, phone, location, hourly pay rate, and **clock PIN**.
- **Assign PINs** — gives a new 6-digit PIN to anyone who does not have one yet. If you assign a new PIN, the old one stops working; tell the employee the new number.
- The profile also shows their schedule, recent punches, and hours for the current pay week.

## Timecards

Review hours and pay for the current **pay week** (Monday through Sunday).

- The main list shows everyone: scheduled hours, regular hours, overtime, total hours, and estimated pay.
- Tap column headers to sort.
- **Download CSV** saves a spreadsheet of what you are viewing.
- Tap a person to see each scheduled shift that week, then tap a shift to view or correct clock in, clock out, and break time.
- **Status** on each shift:
  - **OK** — punched time matches the schedule (within about 15 minutes of paid hours)
  - **Review** — hours do not match or something needs a look
  - **Open** — still clocked in
  - **No punch** — scheduled but no punch recorded yet for that day

Regular pay covers scheduled shift time (rounded to 15 minutes). Any time worked **beyond** the scheduled shift is overtime at **1.5×** the hourly rate.

## Actions

- **Requests** — time-off and other requests submitted by employees. Review and filter the list here.
- **Callouts** — log of coverage outreach from the schedule.

## Messages

Send and read messages with employees. Search to find a conversation, then open a thread to reply.

## Setting up the time clock tablet

The wall tablet is not signed in as an employee.

1. On the login page, tap **Time clock tablet sign in**.
2. Sign in with name **`iPad`** and password **`redpoke`**.
3. Leave the tablet on the punch screen. Staff only enter their **6-digit PIN** (see list at the end of this guide).
4. First PIN entry of the day = **clock in**. Second entry = **clock out**.
5. **Breaks:** tap **Start break** or **End break**, enter PIN, and confirm. Break times appear in Timecards for managers.

# For employees

Sign in with your **full roster name** (for example `MARK ONG`) and password **`redpoke`**.

You will see three tabs: **Home**, **Messages**, and **Actions**.

## Home

- See **today’s shift** (if you are scheduled).
- Browse **upcoming shifts** by week using **Prev week** / **Next week**.

## Messages

Read and send messages to managers.

## Actions

Submit requests such as time off. Fill out the form and submit; managers see it under **Actions → Requests**.

## Clocking in and out at work

You do **not** need to sign into the employee app to punch the time clock.

1. Go to the shared tablet on the time clock screen.
2. Enter your **6-digit PIN** (see your manager or the list below).
3. The screen will confirm **Clocked in** or **Clocked out**.
4. If it says the PIN is not recognized, ask a manager to confirm your PIN on **Team → your name**.

Times are rounded to the nearest **5 minutes** for payroll.
