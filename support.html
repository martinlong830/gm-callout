Support — Shiflow

Last updated: June 2026

Shiflow is a workplace app for restaurant staff and managers at Red Poke. Use it to view schedules, request time off or shift swaps, exchange messages with your team, and manage timecards and clock punches.

How to get help

Most questions are handled by your on-site manager or employer administrator. They can update roster access, correct punch times, and approve schedule requests.

  • Schedule or timecard questions: contact your manager.
  • Login issues: confirm your username and password with your manager. Accounts are created by your employer.
  • Password reset: use Forgot password? on the Shiflow sign-in screen. A reset link is sent to the email on file for your account (if configured by your employer).
